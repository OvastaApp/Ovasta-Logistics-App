Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 31, 2025

Ovasta ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Ovasta Logistics App. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and phone number provided during registration.
  • Location Data: Precise (GPS) and approximate location, including background location, to enable delivery tracking and route optimization.
  • Device Information: Device identifiers for push notification delivery via Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and manage delivery logistics services.
  • To track delivery routes and assign tasks to drivers.
  • To send push notifications about delivery assignments and status updates.
  • To improve our services and user experience.
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud.

3. Background Location Usage

Our app collects location data in the background to enable real-time delivery tracking even when the app is not in the foreground. This is essential for:

  • Providing accurate delivery ETAs to customers.
  • Optimizing delivery routes in real time.
  • Ensuring driver safety and accountability during active deliveries.

Background location is only collected during active delivery sessions.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, push notifications, and analytics.
  • Service Partners: Limited delivery information shared with customers (e.g., driver location during active delivery).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Location data from completed deliveries is retained for up to 90 days for dispute resolution, then automatically deleted.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), secure server infrastructure, and access controls to protect your information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for location tracking (note: this may limit app functionality).

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
